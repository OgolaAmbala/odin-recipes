How to make Macaroni

Lasagna is one of those dishes that looks time consuming but with enough care and dedication, you can make the most amazing dish. Below are a detailed list of ingredients with step-by-step instructions.

You start off by boiling the pasta in a large pot of salted water. Next you make the meat sauce which consists of sausage, beef, onion and garlic. You then add the pasta to the sauce and drain well

  • Sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • One onion and two cloves of garlic
  • One can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce and two cans of tomato paste
  • Two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
